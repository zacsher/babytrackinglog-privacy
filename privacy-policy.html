Baby Tracking Log

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Overview

Baby Tracking Log ("the App") is developed by Zachary Sher. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the App's functionality. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information We Collect

When you use Baby Tracking Log, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your email address (or an Apple-provided relay address) and a unique user identifier.
  • Baby data: Information you enter about your baby, including name, sleep logs (start and end times, nap or bedtime), feeding logs (type, duration, amount), and diaper logs (type and notes).
  • Subscription status: Whether you have an active premium subscription. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple — we never receive or store your payment or credit card information.
  • Device and usage data: Firebase may automatically collect basic analytics data such as device type, operating system version, app version, and crash reports to help us improve the App.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality (logging and displaying your baby's sleep, feeding, and diaper data)
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Manage your subscription status
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve the App's performance

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which provides industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Firestore security rules ensure that you can only access your own data — no other user can view or modify your information.

While we implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Your data may be processed by the following service providers solely to operate the App:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, data storage, and analytics
  • Apple: Sign in with Apple authentication and subscription payment processing

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below. Upon receiving a deletion request, we will remove your data within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Baby Tracking Log is designed for parents and caregivers to track their baby's activities. The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The baby data entered in the App is provided by the parent or caregiver, not by the child.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
